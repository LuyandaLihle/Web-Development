GPS coordinates

Directions

From Durban city centre

Go down either Dr AB Xuma (Commercial) Street or Doctor Pixley Kaseme (West) Street towards the beachfront
Turn LEFT into Stalwart Simelane (Stanger) Street, travelling north
At Sandile Thusi (Argyle) Road, turn LEFT
Going away from the sea, cross over the railway bridge, then cross Umgeni Road
Turn RIGHT into Matthews Meyewa (Stamford Hill) Road
Turn first LEFT into Steel Road
Turn first LEFT into Henwood Road
MIET AFRICA is halfway down, on the right (#59)

From King Shaka International Airport and the N2 (then on the M4, via Umhlanga)

From the airport, get on the N2 going SOUTH
Take Exit 182 (Mt Edgecombe and Umhlanga), and keep LEFT
At the traffic lights, turn LEFT and proceed towards the sea
Follow the road, which will loop round and merge onto the M4, southbound
Continue along for about 8km until the first set of traffic lights, and turn RIGHT into Sandile Thusi (Argyle) Road
Going away from the sea, cross over the railway bridge, and then cross Umgeni Road
Turn RIGHT into Matthews Meyewa (Stamford Hill) Road
Turn first LEFT into Steel Road
Turn first LEFT into Henwood Road
MIET AFRICA is halfway down, on the right (#59)

From the N3 travelling eastwards from Pinetown From the N3, take the Berea Exit*
Turn LEFT into Botanic Gardens Road
Follow the road (it twists and changes its name a few times)
Turn RIGHT into Sandile Thusi (Argyle) Rd, and proceed towards the sea
After passing Lillian Nygoye (Windermere) Road, turn LEFT into Henwood Road
MIET AFRICA is halfway down, on the left (#59)

*  Note

. If you miss this turning, proceed into town, and then follow the
directions from Durban city centre (above)
